Brother Hunt

 

A 3D Game about a particle running inside the Large Hadron Collider at CERN, trying to figure out the mystery of what happened to its brother. This game is inspired by the game called Temple Run, and aims to be played by small children and high school children to subconsciously introduce them to basic ideas of Particle Physics.

This was built as a part of the CERN Summer Student Webfest 2014, and it was awarded the best entry in the education track. Yayyyyy !!
